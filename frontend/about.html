About Visible2AI

Be Found. Be Recommended. Be Chosen.

Our Mission

Visible2AI helps businesses become discoverable, understandable, and recommendable by AI assistants — so your ideal customers find you first and choose you over competitors.

In a world where buyers discover brands through ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini, we give companies a clear, actionable path to becoming the answer AI recommends.

If AI doesn't understand your business, it can't recommend you. And if AI doesn't recommend you, your customers choose your competitors.

The Shift Is Here

The way buyers find businesses has fundamentally changed.

68% of B2B buyers now use AI assistants for research instead of Google. When they ask "Who's the best [service] in [location]?" — AI gives one answer, not ten links.

But here's the problem: most websites are invisible to AI.

Not because they're bad — but because AI can't interpret their content, verify their credibility, or extract clear answers. Traditional SEO doesn't work. AI uses completely different signals.

There's no page two of ChatGPT. You're either the answer, or you're forgotten.

  • 8-Pillar Analysis — breakdown across 47 factors that determine AI visibility
  • Prioritized Recommendations — what to fix first for maximum impact
  • Implementation-Ready Code — copy-paste fixes, no developer required

We don't just identify problems. We solve them.

Our Story

It started with a pattern we couldn't ignore.

In a single week, three different clients called with the same panic: "Our SEO traffic is down 30%, our leads are garbage, and prospects keep mentioning they found competitors through ChatGPT. What do we do?"

We knew what was happening — high-intent B2B buyers were shifting from Google to AI assistants for research. But traditional SEO tools offered no solutions. Every framework, every methodology was built for a world where people clicked through search results, not one where AI delivered single answers.

We needed to understand how people who've never known search without AI actually behave.

That's when our technical co-founder stepped forward with a different perspective — and a solution.

Built by Someone Who's Never Known Search Without AI

Arhan Supramanyam, our 18-year-old technical co-founder and computer science student, represents a generation with fundamentally different search behavior.

"When everyone kept talking about 'AI search,' I was confused. For me, there's no 'AI search' versus 'regular search' — there's just search. I've never typed a question into Google and scrolled through 10 blue links. When I need to find a restaurant, research a product, or discover a service provider, I ask ChatGPT, Claude, or Siri. I get one answer, maybe two or three options. That's it.

And here's what matters: I'm not an early adopter or a tech enthusiast. This is just normal for everyone I know. My friends, my classmates — we all search this way. If your business isn't structured for AI to understand and recommend you, you literally don't exist in my world.

We're not the future. We're your customers right now."

Rather than retrofitting old SEO frameworks for a new reality, Arhan built Visible2AI from first principles — designed for how people actually search today, not how they searched in 2019.
