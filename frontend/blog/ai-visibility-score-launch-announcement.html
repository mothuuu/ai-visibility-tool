The Problem: 94% of Websites Are Invisible to AI

When someone asks an AI assistant for a recommendation, it provides a direct answer. You're either that answer, or you're forgotten.

Our research reveals that 94% of websites lack the technical foundation, content structure, and authority signals that AI systems need to confidently recommend them. These businesses are effectively invisible to the growing number of consumers who turn to AI for purchase decisions.
