Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2024

1. Introduction

Visible2AI ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password
  • Business Profile: Business name, website URL, description, category, contact information, address, logo, social media links
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe; we do not store full credit card numbers

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, scan history
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, operating system
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication, preference cookies

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Directory Responses: Confirmation of listings, verification status
  • OAuth Providers: If you sign in with Google/LinkedIn, we receive basic profile information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process your AI visibility scans and generate recommendations
  • Submit your business profile to directories (AI Citation Network)
  • Handle verification emails and directory account management
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send you updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Improve our Service through analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Citation Network: Data Handling

When you use the AI Citation Network feature:

4.1 Business Information Distribution

Your business profile information is shared with third-party directories for the purpose of creating listings. Each directory has its own privacy policy governing how they use your information.

4.2 Managed Inboxes

If you choose a managed inbox, we create an email address (e.g., yourcompany@listings.visible2ai.com) for directory communications. We store email metadata (sender, subject, timestamp) but do not permanently store email body content.

4.3 Credential Storage

Directory account credentials are encrypted using AES-256-GCM and stored in our secure credential vault. Access is audit-logged. You may export or request deletion of credentials at any time.

4.4 Data Retention

  • Verification email metadata: 90 days
  • Submission records: 2 years
  • Evidence (screenshots, hashes): 1 year
  • Deleted credentials: Purged within 30 days

5. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Directories: Your business profile for listing purposes
  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe), email services, cloud hosting (encrypted data only)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure credential storage with audit logging
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Secure development practices

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Credential Handoff: Export directory credentials and take ownership

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@visible2ai.com

8. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Authentication, security, session management
  • Functional: Remembering preferences
  • Analytics: Understanding usage patterns (anonymized)

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including standard contractual clauses where applicable.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the Service. The "Last Updated" date indicates when the policy was last revised.
